About Motionly

AI-native motion graphics, kept editable.

Motionly is an AI motion graphics editor for creating customizable animation projects from prompts, assets, and visual edits. It helps turn ideas into readable .motion source files that can be refined on a canvas, adjusted on a timeline, and exported to MP4.

The problem

Teams often need short product videos, feature launch animations, social clips, and SVG motion graphics that feel specific to their product. Motionly focuses on making those AI-generated animation drafts editable instead of final and frozen.

The workflow

Start with an AI-generated draft, inspect the result visually, move layers on the canvas, tune timing on the timeline, then keep iterating in source. The project stays local and version-friendly.
